About EcoBhoomi Enterprise

Building Reliable Supply Chains for Rural & Semi-Urban Assam

Founded with a single mission — to bring trusted, high-quality FMCG and beverage products within easy reach of every retailer in Assam — EcoBhoomi Enterprise has grown into a dependable regional super stockist headquartered in Barpeta district.

We bridge the gap between national/regional brands and the last-mile retail ecosystem, ensuring on-time delivery, transparent pricing and consistent product availability for distributors, wholesalers and small retail outlets.

Our team operates with a single ethos: respect the land that feeds us. From responsible logistics to fair partnerships with local vendors, we work every day to keep our communities growing, hydrated and energized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

What does EcoBhoomi Enterprise do?

EcoBhoomi Enterprise is a regional FMCG and soft-drink super stockist based in Barpeta, Assam. We distribute Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Sting, Bohagi, Frego, Kenpo, Frizee and packaged water to wholesalers and retailers across Assam.

Which areas in Assam do you cover?

We cover Barpeta, Howly, Sarthebari, Pathsala, Bajali, Baksa, Tamulpur and Mushalpur with extended coverage into Nalbari district. Need delivery in a town not listed? Get in touch — we’re expanding constantly.

How can a retailer become a partner?

Retailers can call +91 74061 13544 or email contact@ecobhoomi.in to begin onboarding. We offer flexible commercial terms, on-time delivery and trade marketing support.

Are you an authorized distributor for Sira Agro Foods?

Yes. EcoBhoomi Enterprise is an authorized super stockist of Sira Agro Foods Pvt. Ltd., supplying Bohagi, Frego, Kenpo and Frizee brands.

What products do you offer?

Our catalog includes carbonated soft drinks, energy drinks, juice beverages, flavored masala drinks like Bohagi Jeera Masala and Nimbu Pani, and packaged drinking water in multiple SKUs.

Do you support credit terms for retailers?

Yes, we extend flexible credit terms based on order volume and engagement history. New retailers typically begin with cash-and-carry and graduate to credit as the relationship matures.
